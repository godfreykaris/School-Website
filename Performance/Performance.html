Kagumo High School is a high performance school producing top students in the country. In the last KCSE, 2020, It produced the best underage student who scored an A. This was pointed out the Education CS Prof. George Magoha in the results briefing just before they were released to the students. He personally acknowledged that the school produces some of the sharpest minds in the country.

The teachers together with the support staff in the school work extremely hard to make sure that the students perform their best.

The students' performance is also as a result of the high discipline portrayed by the students in and out of the school premises. The school is proud to always being top 100 in the country with the position being top 20 in the 2020 KCSE exam.
