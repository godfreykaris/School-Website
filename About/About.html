About Kagumo High School

Kagumo High School is a boy's national secondary school located between Kirichu and Kiganjo townships at Kagumo near Karatina on the Nyeri-Nanyuki road in Kenya. It was one of the first schools in Kenya to allow native black Africans to sit for university level entrance exams, doing so in 1946.

It is one of the best high schools in Kenya, with prominent old boys in Kenya and globally. It has several notable alumni including lawyers, doctors and politicians such as the constitutional lawyer Gibson Kamau Kuria.

In academics, sports and creative arts this school has a long-standing rivalry with Nyeri High School, with meetings between the two schools, particularly in rugby, and basketball the most intense.

To achieve this, I appeal for support from all stakeholders -of the school. I assure you that everyone has a place in making the school great and any contributions in this endeavor is welcome.

Our first goal is academic excellence. All other activities are meant to serve the results our students get in examinations.
